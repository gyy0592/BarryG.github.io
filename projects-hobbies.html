Custom CPU Water Cooling System

In a creative approach to PC cooling, I designed a portable and cost-effective water cooling system. By soldering a hollow copper tube directly onto my CPU and connecting it to a pump and a large mineral water bottle, I created a unique cooling solution that's both efficient and mobile.
